# 🧙‍♂️ The Magic Guide (Read Me!)

> [!tip] Welcome to the Master Class

---

### 🌟 Master Quest 1: The Infinite Canvas (Scrivenings)
*Tired of switching between files to read your own story? Let's stitch them together.*
👉 **Action 1:** Make sure you have this note (or any chapter) open.
👉 **Action 2:** Look at the right panel and click the big blue **"📚 Scrivenings"** button.
👉 **Action 3:** Select "Select All" and click Confirm. 
✨ **Magic:** Watch as Chapter 1 and Chapter 2 instantly merge into a single, seamless draft! You can now edit across chapters without friction.

---

### 🌟 Master Quest 2: The God Mode (Cross-Chapter Drag & Drop)
*You realized a scene in Chapter 2 actually belongs in Chapter 1. Cut and paste? No. We do it the elegant way.*
👉 **Action 1:** While you are still inside the "Scrivenings Draft" from Quest 1, open the **"📑 Outline"** tab in the right panel.
👉 **Action 2:** Find the scene `🎬 Infiltration` under Chapter 2.
👉 **Action 3:** Click and **drag it upwards**, dropping it right between the two scenes in Chapter 1!
✨ **Magic:** The text in the editor instantly shifts. But wait, it gets better...
👉 **Action 4:** Click the red **"💾 Sync & Close"** button at the top of the panel. Go check your original `Chapter 1.md` and `Chapter 2.md` files. The text has physically moved between the files!

---

### 🌟 Master Quest 3: The World Builder (Auto-Wiki)
*Notice those glowing `[[Internal Links]]` like OmniCorp and Sector 4? Let's build a database out of them automatically.*
👉 **Action 1:** Open `Chapter 1 - The Neon Rain`.
👉 **Action 2:** Click **"🛠️ Tools -> 🧠 Auto Wiki"** in the right panel.
✨ **Magic:** A notification will pop up. Now, go open the `02_📚 World Bible` folder in your file explorer. Your lore database has just built itself!

---

### 🌟 Master Quest 4: The Publisher (Export & Compile)
*Your masterpiece is complete, but it's full of system tags, notes, and internal links. How do you get a clean version for your readers or publisher?*
👉 **Action 1:** Make sure you are viewing a normal chapter (not the Scrivenings draft). 
👉 **Action 2:** Look at the right panel and click the **"📤 Compile"** button at the top.
👉 **Action 3:** A window appears. Select both Chapter 1 and Chapter 2, then click Next.
👉 **Action 4:** Now, select the messy drafting marks you want to strip away (e.g., Scene Info, Comments, Internal Links). Click **"Compile Now"**.
✨ **Magic:** NovelSmith instantly creates a perfectly clean, continuous manuscript in the `04_📤 Published Output` folder (or your default Output folder). All your backstage tags, IDs, and messy notes are stripped away automatically. You are ready to publish!
