# ✍️ Welcome to NovelSmith Forge!

> [!tip] What is this Vault?
> This is a fully configured, interactive sandbox for **NovelSmith** — a plugin designed specifically for long-form fiction and serial web novel writers.
> 
> Everything is set up. Feel free to break things, delete texts, and push buttons. That's what the sandbox is for!
---
### 🚀 Step 1: Ignite the Engine
To start your journey, look at the **Left Ribbon** (the vertical icon bar on the far left) and click the 📖 **Book Icon** to open the **NovelSmith Console** on your right pane.
*(If you prefer shortcuts, press `Cmd/Ctrl + P` and search for "Open Structure Outline".)*

---
### 🗺️ Step 2: Choose Your Quest
Where would you like to go first?
- **[ 📚 The Scrivenings Experience ]** Go to `01_📖 Book One - Neon Resonance`. Open any chapter and click the **"📚 Scrivenings"** button in the right panel. Experience seamless, infinite-scroll writing across multiple files!
- **[ 🎯 The Training Grounds ]** Want to test the black magic? Head over to the `03_🎯 Training Grounds` folder. We have prepared broken drafts for you to test out the **Typo Police**, **Atomic Backups**, and **Card Splitting**.
- **[ 🧠 The Auto-Wiki Magic ]** See how world-building is automated. Open any chapter with `[[Internal Links]]` and click **"🛠️ Tools -> 🧠 Auto Wiki"** in the right panel. Watch your World Bible build itself.
---
### 🏁 Ready to write your own masterpiece?
1. Copy this entire vault folder to your local drive.
2. Delete the demo folders (`01`, `02`, `03`, `04`).
3. Set your own Book Folder in the Plugin Settings.
4. **Start writing!**
